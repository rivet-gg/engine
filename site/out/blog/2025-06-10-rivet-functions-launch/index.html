Rivet Functions Launch

Announcing Rivet Functions – open-source serverless for your backend logic.

Key Features

  • Multi-language Support: Supports Node.js, Bun, Python, & Rust
  • Real-time Capabilities: Serve realtime workloads with WebSockets
  • No Timeouts: Run long-running processes without artificial limits
  • Edge Computing: Runs close to your users for optimal performance
